Queso Chicken Chili

This Queso Chicken Chili is creamy, cheesy, and filling. It’s perfect for a cozy dinner and easy to make in one pot.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast (or shredded rotisserie chicken)
  • 1 can black beans (drained and rinsed)
  • 1 can corn (drained)
  • 1 jar queso dip (or queso blanco)
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 can diced tomatoes with green chiles

Steps

  1. Add chicken broth and diced tomatoes to a pot.
  2. Add chicken and bring to a gentle simmer.
  3. Cook until chicken is done, then shred it (if using raw chicken).
  4. Stir in black beans and corn.
  5. Mix in the queso and stir until creamy and hot.
  6. Serve warm (optional: top with chips, jalapeños, or cilantro).
